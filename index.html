Welcome to Sharedle!

Sharedle is a replica of the popular game created by Josh Wardle: Wordle, a game in which you attempt to find a mystery five-letter word in six guesses or less.

The rules are exaclty the same here, Sharedle just provides a few extra features that Wordle does not.

  • The ability to play more than once a day, allowing you to hone your skills.
  • The ability to solve a Wordle and send it to your friends for them to complete it too.
  • The ability to create your own custom Wordle and send it to your friends to solve.
